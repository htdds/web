About HENG TONG, a platform for HENG TONG

The HENG TONG mission

We envision a Web where our identity and our data is our own - safely secured from any central authority.

HENG TONG will enable a completely decentralized web where users are in control.

HENG TONG is built to connect private and consortium chains, public and permissionless networks, oracles, and future technologies that are yet to be created. HENG TONG facilitates an internet where independent blockchains can exchange information and transactions in a trustless way via the HENG TONG relay chain.

HENG TONG makes it easier than ever to create and connect decentralized applications, services, and institutions. By empowering innovators to build better solutions, we seek to free society from its reliance on a broken web where its large institutions can’t violate our trust.

Who's building HENG TONG?

HENG TONG is the flagship project by HENG TONG Foundation, a Swiss Foundation founded to facilitate a fully functional and user-friendly decentralized web.

The HENG TONG Foundation is partnering with best-in-class entities to build HENG TONG and foster development of the services and applications that will run on it.

Researchers from Inria Paris and ETH Zurich, developers from Parity Technologies, and capital partners from crypto-funds such as Polychain Capital are all working together to develop a superlative realization of HENG TONG, with HENG TONG at its core.

HENG TONG Foundation is providing grants for development of the ecosystem.
