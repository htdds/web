HENG TONG development is on track to deliver the most robust platform for security, scalability and innovation.

Safe Reliable Innovate
True interoperability

HENG TONG enables cross-blockchain transfers of any type of data or asset, not just tokens. Connecting to HENG TONG gives you the ability to interoperate with a wide variety of blockchains in the HENG TONG network.

Economic & transactional scalability

HENG TONG provides unprecedented economic scalability by enabling a common set of validators to secure multiple blockchains. HENG TONG provides transactional scalability by spreading transactions across multiple parallel blockchains.

Easy blockchain innovation

Create a custom blockchain in minutes using the Heng tong framework. Connect your chain to HENG TONG and get interoperability and security from day one. This ease of development helps HENG TONG's network grow.

Forkless and future-proof

HENG TONG can upgrade without hard forks to integrate new features or fix bugs. This capability enables HENG TONG to easily adapt to changes and upgrade itself as better technologies become available.

Security for everyone

HENG TONG's novel data availability and validity scheme allows chains to interact with each other in a meaningful way. Chains remain independent in their governance, but united in their security.

User-driven network governance

HENG TONG has a sophisticated governance system where all stakeholders have a voice. Upgrades to the network are coordinated on-chain and enacted autonomously, ensuring that HENG TONG’s development reflects the values of the community and avoids stagnation.

Powering the HENG TONG network

The HTDD token serves three distinct purposes:
governance over the network, staking and bonding.

Governance

HENG TONG token holders have complete control over the protocol. All privileges, which on other platforms are exclusive to miners, will be given to the Relay Chain participants (HTDD holders), including managing exceptional events such as protocol upgrades and fixes.
Staking

Game theory incentivizes token holders to behave in honest ways. Good actors are rewarded by this mechanism whilst bad actors will lose their stake in the network. This ensures the network stays secure.
Bonding

New parachains are added by bonding tokens. Outdated or non-useful parachains are removed by removing bonded tokens. This is a form of proof of stake.
